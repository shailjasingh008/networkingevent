When a lion roars, only other lions can understand.

Similarly, if you want investors to understand your idea and give you an opportunity to pitch, then you should have mastered the fundamentals of finance as it will equip you to communicate effectively using the language of business – numbers!

In this masterclass, you will learn:

5:00 pm – 6:15 pm The fundamentals of a balance sheet, profit & loss statement, and cash flow statement

6:15 pm – 6:45 pm The difference between profit & cash, debit & credit, expense & asset

6:45 pm – 7:30 pm Break-even point and contribution margin

7:30 pm – 8:15 pm Budgeting & Forecasting

8:15 pm – 9:00 pm Understanding the terms ROI, ROE, ROA & ROCE with examples

After this masterclass, participants will have a new perspective on the subject finance and will never again shy away from numbers, instead will be able to confidently interact with financial experts and investors.

Who should attend? This masterclass is designed for startup founders in the pre-revenue stage, the second-generation family business owners and wannabe entrepreneurs.

All attendees will get lifetime access to the online video program “Business Finance Essentials” and will get a hard copy of the bestselling book - FUNdamentals of Financial Statements.

(The book will be a hardcopy and will be couriered to the participant provided they take care of the courier charges. Usually, it is between Rs 40 - 100 max)
